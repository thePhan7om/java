*Selection Sort

- The Selection Sort Algorthium sorts an array by repeatedly finding the smallest element
in the unsorted tail section

- [0][1][2][3][4]
   11 9 17  5  12
   
 	- First step find smallest element in this case 5 in element [3], move 5 to the begining
 	- need to swap with 11
 - [0][1][2][3][4]
    5  9 17 11 12
    
	- repeat until sorted
